Week 3: Dashboard Report

Objective

To create a dashboard for analyzing retail sales data.

Dashboard Components

- Top 10 Customers by Sales
- Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
- Total Sales KPI (2.30M)

Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel / WPS Office
- Pivot Tables
- Charts

Key Insights

- Consumer segment has highest sales
- Top customers contribute significantly to revenue
- Total sales is approximately 2.30 million

Conclusion

The dashboard provides a clear and simple visualization of sales performance and helps in better decision-making.
