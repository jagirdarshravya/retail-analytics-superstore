Week 4: Insight Analysis and Final Submission
During Week 4,

I concentrated on analyzing the dashboard that I created and understanding the data in a deeper way. The aim was not just to view the charts, but to interpret the data and identify meaningful patterns in sales and profit.

Insights from Dashboard:

Sales performance is higher in the Consumer segment compared to other segments
Profit is stronger in the West region, indicating better performance in that area
Among all categories, Technology contributes the most to profit
Some regions and categories show lower profit, which indicates areas that need attention
The trend of sales and profit is not stable and varies over time
Applying filters like region, category, and segment helps in analyzing specific data clearly

Recommendations:

More focus should be given to high-performing regions to increase overall profit
Low-performing categories need to be analyzed to identify the reasons behind poor performance
Business decisions can be improved by considering region-wise and category-wise results
Continuous monitoring of trends can help in avoiding sudden decline in performance
Steps should be taken to minimize losses from low-performing products
