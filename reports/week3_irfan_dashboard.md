Week 3: Profit Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

During Week 3, I worked on building an interactive Profit Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to better understand the overall profit performance from the dataset.

Work Done:
- Created a KPI card to highlight the total profit value for quick overview
- Developed a region-wise bar chart to compare profit across different regions
- Built a line chart to visualize profit trends over time and identify patterns
- Designed a donut chart to represent profit contribution by each category
- Added interactive slicers for Order Date, Category, and Region to allow dynamic filtering of data

Key Insights:
- The West region stands out as the most profitable region among all
- Profit trend shows variation over time, with an overall decreasing pattern in later periods
- The Technology category contributes the highest share of profit
- Some regions and categories generate comparatively lower profit, indicating possible areas to focus on

Conclusion:
The dashboard provides a clear and interactive way to analyze profit performance. By using different visualizations and filters, it becomes easier to identify trends, compare segments, and gain meaningful insights for decision-making.
