Week 3: Sales Dashboard(Power BI)

In Week 3, I created a sales dashboard using Power BI to understand the data in a visual way.

What I did:
- I created a KPI card to show total sales
- I made a bar chart to compare sales across categories
- I created a line chart to see how sales change over time
- I added a chart to show sales in different regions
- I created a chart to identify top selling products
- I added filters (slicers) for region and month to make the dashboard interactive

Insights:
- Technology category has the highest sales
- Sales change over time and show some variation
- West region has higher sales compared to others
- Some products are selling more than others

Conclusion:

This dashboard helps to understand sales performance easily. By using charts and filters, it becomes easy to analyze data and find useful insights.This dashboard can help businesses make better decisions.
